Welcome! I am a first-year PhD candidate at LIP6 - Sorbonne Université, specializing in computational social choice and fair allocation. My research lies at the intersection of multiagent systems, decision theory, and social choice. Previously, I completed my master's degree in operations research at Université Paris-Dauphine-PSL.

CONTACT: maxime.lucet@lip6.fr

Research Interests:

Fair Allocation

My research focuses on developing efficient algorithms and theoretical frameworks for fair allocation problems in multiagent systems.

Computational Social Choice

Investigating the computational aspects of collective decision-making and social choice mechanisms.

Decision Theory

Using discrete mathematics to design algorithms involving human decision making.
